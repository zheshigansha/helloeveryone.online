Diamant: Sky Stories Match 3

An exciting online game where you match gems and explore the sky!

Game Overview

Diamant: Sky Stories Match 3 is a captivating puzzle game that invites players to match colorful gems in a beautiful sky-themed environment. Use your skills to complete levels and uncover the mysteries of the sky!

How to Play

To play, simply swap adjacent gems to create a line of three or more matching gems. The more gems you match, the higher your score! Complete the objectives for each level to progress through the game.
