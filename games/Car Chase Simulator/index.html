Mr. Drifter: Car Chase Simulator

An exciting online game where you experience thrilling car chases!

Game Overview

Mr. Drifter: Car Chase Simulator is an exhilarating game that puts you in the driver's seat of high-speed car chases. Navigate through traffic, evade the police, and complete daring missions in this action-packed adventure!

How to Play

Use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to steer your car. Avoid obstacles and outrun your pursuers to complete each level. The faster you drive, the more points you earn!
