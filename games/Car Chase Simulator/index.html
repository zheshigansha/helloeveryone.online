How to Play

In Car Chase Simulator, you'll take on the role of an experienced driver, performing various chase missions around the city. Your goal is to evade police cars or pursue target vehicles, all while avoiding obstacles and traffic. Challenge your reflexes and driving skills to become the ultimate king of the road!

Controls:

  • Movement: Use keyboard W, A, S, D keys or the Arrow Keys to control your vehicle's forward, backward, and steering movements.
  • Accelerate/Brake: W key to accelerate, S key to brake/reverse.
  • Handbrake: Press Space bar for a handbrake turn/drift.
  • Camera View: Some versions might support C key to switch camera views.
  • Mission Objectives: Your current mission objectives, such as escape time, collecting items, or reaching a specific location, will be displayed on the game interface.
  • Avoid Obstacles: Be careful to avoid other vehicles, buildings, and obstacles on the road, as they can slow you down or cause damage.

Master these basic controls to navigate the intense and thrilling chases in Car Chase Simulator! Good luck!
