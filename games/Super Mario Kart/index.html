How to Play

Welcome to the wonderful world of Super Mario Kart! Choose your favorite Mario character, drive your kart, and race against opponents on tracks filled with power-ups and traps. Your goal is to be the first to cross the finish line, utilizing various items to hinder your rivals or gain an advantage!

Controls:

  • Movement: Use the keyboard Arrow Keys (Up, Down, Left, Right) or W, A, S, D keys to control your kart's movement.
  • Accelerate/Brake: ↑ or W key to accelerate, ↓ or S key to brake/reverse.
  • Use Item: Collect question mark boxes on the track to get random items. Typically use Space bar or Ctrl key to use items.
  • Drift: While turning, combine a directional key with a specific key (e.g., Shift or Z, depending on emulator settings) to drift and get a mini-turbo boost.
  • Camera View: Some emulator versions might support the C key to switch between different camera views.
  • Avoid Obstacles: Be mindful of various obstacles and traps on the track, such as banana peels and Koopa Shells. Avoid them to prevent slowing down or being spun out.

Master these techniques to race through the circuits of Super Mario Kart and enjoy endless fun! Compete with friends and see who truly is the King of Karting!
