Super Mario Kart

An exciting online racing game where you race against friends and foes!

Game Overview

Super Mario Kart is a thrilling racing game where you can race against friends and foes on vibrant tracks. Use power-ups to gain an advantage and be the first to cross the finish line!

How to Play

Use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to steer your kart. Collect power-ups to boost your speed and hinder your opponents!
